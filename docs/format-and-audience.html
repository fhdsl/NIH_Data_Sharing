Format and Audience

0.1 Course Format

This course is available in Bookdown and Leanpub formats. The Bookdown format allows you to browse the content on a web page. Creating a free account with Leanpub allows you to take quizzes on the material and earn a certificate of completion.

0.2 Motivation

We want to help make the process of compliance with the new NIH Data Management and Sharing Policy https://sharing.nih.gov/ easier.

0.3 Target Audience

The course is intended for anyone who plans to submit a grant proposal to the NIH after January 25th, 2023.
